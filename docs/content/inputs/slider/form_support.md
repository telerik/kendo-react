# Forms Support

The Slider component can be easily integrated with our [KendoReact Form]({% slug overview_form %}) as well as the [HTML5 form](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Learn/HTML/Forms) (or other third-party solutions).

The form enables you to validate the slider and prevent the submission of forms which are in an invalid state.
