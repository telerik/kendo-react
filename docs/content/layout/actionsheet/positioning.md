# Positioning

The ActionSheet enables you to specify its position in relation to the page content.

To specify where the ActionSheet will be displayed, use the [`position`]({% slug api_layout_actionsheetprops %}#toc-position) property.

The ActionSheet provides the following options for positioning:

-   `top`&mdash;Positions the ActionSheet at the top side of the screen.
-   `bottom`(Default)&mdash;Positions the ActionSheet at the bottom side of the screen.
-   `left`&mdash;Positions the ActionSheet at the left side of the screen.
-   `right`&mdash;Positions the ActionSheet at the right side of the screen.
-   `fullsceen`&mdash;Positions the ActionSheet on full size of the screen.
